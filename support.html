Getting Started

How do I download a model?

Tap the model icon (layers icon, top left) to open the model list. Tap "Download" next to any model. Gemma 2 2B (1.6 GB) is the fastest to download and works great for most tasks.

Which iPhone do I need?

iPhone 13 or newer is recommended (6 GB RAM). The app runs on any iPhone with iOS 17+. iPhone 15 Pro and newer provide the best performance (40+ tokens/sec).

How do I start chatting?

After a model downloads, tap "Load" in the model list. Once loaded (shown in the title bar), type your message and tap Send.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work offline?

Yes! After the initial model download, everything runs 100% offline. No internet connection required.

Why are model files so large?

AI language models contain billions of compressed parameters. Smaller models (1–2 GB) are fast and capable. Larger models (3–5 GB) provide higher quality responses.

What is Pro?

Pro unlocks: unlimited model switching, unlimited chat history, custom system prompts, and the HuggingFace model browser. The free tier includes 1 model and limited chats.

How do I restore my purchase?

Tap the ⋯ menu (top right) → Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

The model output looks wrong or garbled

Delete the model (swipe left in the model list → Delete) and re-download it. If the issue persists, try a different model.

How do I delete a downloaded model?

Open the model list → swipe left on an installed model → tap Delete.
