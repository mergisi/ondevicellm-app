Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

OnDevice LLM ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

No Data Collection

OnDevice LLM does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. All AI inference runs entirely on your device. Your conversations, prompts, and model usage are never sent to any server.

How It Works

The app runs open-source AI models (in GGUF format) directly on your iPhone using Apple's Metal GPU framework. No internet connection is required after the initial model download. Models are downloaded directly from HuggingFace to your device — no account required.

Third-Party Services

OnDevice LLM uses the following third-party services:

  • HuggingFace - For model file downloads only. No account or login required. Subject to HuggingFace's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit - For managing in-app purchases and subscriptions. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple.

Data Storage

All data (chat history, settings, downloaded models) is stored locally on your device. We have no access to this data. Deleting the app removes all stored data.

Analytics

OnDevice LLM does not use any analytics or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

OnDevice LLM is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at mergisi@gmail.com.
